What it does

Built for instant expression

Key Mapping

Assign any audio file on your device to any key — A through Z or 0 through 9. Your board, your sounds.

Instant Playback

Zero-lag tap on the grid or hit a physical Bluetooth keyboard. The sound fires the moment you touch it.

Import & Export

Save your entire key layout as a portable JSON file. Share it, restore it, or back it up to any device.

36 Keys

Full coverage — all 26 letters plus 10 digits. 36 instant sounds, always organised, always one tap away.

Process

Three steps to your soundboard

01

Assign

Open the key manager, choose a key (A–Z or 0–9), browse your audio files, and tap save. Mappings persist across reboots.

02

Play

Tap any key on the home grid or press a physical Bluetooth keyboard. CTunes fires the audio instantly — no menus, no delay.

03

Share

Export your entire layout as a compact JSON file. Import it on another device or keep it as a backup. Your soundboard travels with you.
